Enables you and your customers to login with your e-mail address as well as your username.

== Description ==

By default, you and your customers will have to login using your username. This is not practicall since WooCommerce will auto-generate usernames for your customers and they will often forget what their username is. This plugin will resolve that by enabling your users to login using their e-mail address.

*   Works with WooCommerce
*   Perfect with Klarna Checkout

== Installation ==

It's easy to install this plugin:

1. Upload the content of the zip to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
